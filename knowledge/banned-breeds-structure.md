# Muddy Booking Banned Breeds Structure

## URL and Location
- Main URL: `/settings/banned-breeds`
- Access: Settings > Pricing > Banned breeds
- Navigation: From main settings page, located under "Pricing" section

## Page Structure
- Page title: "Banned breed settings - Management area | Muddy Booking"
- Main heading: "Banned breed settings"

## Policy Options
The page offers 4 radio button policy options:
1. **Allow all** - Allows all breeds (no breed restrictions)
2. **Allow none** - Disallows all banned breeds
3. **Allow some** - Shows breed selection checkboxes when selected
4. **Unspecified** - No specific policy set

## Breed Selection (when "Allow some" is selected)
Shows "Allowed breeds" section with checkboxes for:
- Pit Bull Terrier
- Japanese Tosa
- Dogo Argentino
- Fila Brasileiro
- XL Bully

## Additional Information Field
- Text area labeled "Additional information"
- Placeholder: "Enter your text here..."
- Purpose: Provide more information about banned breeds policy to customers
- Information is made available to customers

## Actions
- **Save button** - Saves configuration and returns to main settings page

## Behavior
- Selecting "Allow some" reveals the breed checkboxes
- Selecting other options hides the breed selection
- Form saves successfully and redirects to main settings page
- Additional information field accepts custom text for customer communication
